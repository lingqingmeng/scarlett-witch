As the creator of **@mandarinwanda**, I built a personal brand around Mandarin language learning and cultural content, growing the account to 36K followers organically in 3 years. Through consistent posting, community engagement, and creative storytelling, I established @mandarinwanda as a trusted resource for learners worldwide.

**Key achievements**
- Averaged ~250K monthly reach and 400K+ monthly views for 3+ years, totaling ~4.1M views and ~191K interactions.
- Maintained a 4.2% average engagement rate through interactive content and reels.
- Partnered with language-learning platforms for sponsored collaborations and brand campaigns.
- Designed all original graphics, reels, and learning materials independently.
- Founded and managed an Etsy e-commerce store selling digital language-learning resources.

**Metrics snapshot**
- Views: 4,144,990  
- Interactions: 191,855  
- Followers: 36K+

Next steps include expanding into longer-form educational video, deeper partnerships with learning platforms, and scaling community-led challenges to sustain engagement.
